RO Pressure Vessels

4-inch, 8-inch & 16-inch FRP RO Membrane Housings

Genoasis is a trusted supplier of FRP (fibreglass reinforced plastic) pressure vessels that house spiral-wound reverse osmosis membrane elements. We stock 4-inch, 8-inch and 16-inch vessels in both side-port and end-port configurations and in 1 to 8 element lengths - so you get the right housing for your array, footprint and piping layout.

Vessels are available in 300, 450, 600 and 1000 psi pressure ratings to match brackish water RO or high-pressure seawater RO duty, and standard FRP vessels are NSF/ANSI 61 certified for drinking water. Send us your membrane count, element size and operating pressure, and our engineers will quote the vessels with matching RO membranes, antiscalants and cartridge filters in the same package.

FRP pressure vessels are supplied across India (Chennai, Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad), the Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait, Bahrain), and Africa (Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Ghana). We respond to every enquiry within 1 hour.

Product Range

FRP Pressure Vessel Types

4-inch RO Pressure Vessels

Compact 4-inch FRP housings for 4040 spiral-wound elements, in 1 to 6 element lengths. Ideal for small industrial and commercial RO skids. Side-port and end-port, rated 300-1000 psi, NSF/ANSI 61 certified.

8-inch RO Pressure Vessels

The industry-standard 8-inch FRP vessels for 8040 elements, in 1 to 8 element lengths. The workhorse of industrial and municipal RO plants. Side-port and end-port, rated 300-1000 psi, NSF/ANSI 61 certified.

16-inch RO Pressure Vessels

Large-diameter 16-inch FRP vessels for high-capacity trains, reducing vessel count, footprint and interconnecting piping on large municipal and desalination RO plants. Side-port, rated up to 600 psi.

Composite & SWRO High-Pressure Vessels

Composite high-pressure vessels rated up to 1000 psi for single-pass seawater desalination (SWRO), plus FRP multimedia and sand filter vessels for RO pre-treatment.

FAQs

FRP Pressure Vessels - Common Questions

Technical answers for RO plant engineers and buyers in India, the UAE and the GCC.

What sizes and configurations of FRP pressure vessels does Genoasis supply?

We supply FRP (fibreglass) RO pressure vessels in 4-inch, 8-inch and 16-inch diameters, in both side-port and end-port configurations and in 1 to 8 element lengths. This lets you house any number of standard spiral-wound RO membrane elements, from small industrial skids to large municipal and desalination trains.

What pressure ratings are available and are the vessels NSF certified?

Our FRP pressure vessels are available in 300, 450, 600 and 1000 psi pressure ratings, so you can match the vessel to brackish water RO or high-pressure seawater RO duty. Standard FRP vessels are NSF/ANSI 61 certified for use in drinking water systems, and composite high-pressure vessels are available for SWRO desalination.

Do you supply high-pressure vessels for seawater desalination?

Yes. For seawater RO (SWRO) desalination we supply composite and high-pressure FRP vessels rated up to 1000 psi to handle the elevated feed pressures of single-pass seawater desalination. We also offer FRP multimedia and sand filter vessels for RO pre-treatment.

Do you supply FRP pressure vessels to the UAE, Saudi Arabia and Africa?

Yes. We supply FRP pressure vessels across the Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait, Bahrain), India, and Africa (Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Ghana, Tanzania, Ethiopia). We handle export documentation and shipping, and respond to enquiries within 1 hour.
