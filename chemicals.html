Water Treatment Chemicals

Specialty chemicals for industrial and municipal water treatment

Genoasis supplies a wide range of Water & Wastewater Treatment Chemicals, including specialty chemicals such as RO Antiscalants, RO Cleaning Chemicals, and Corrosion & Scale Inhibitors, along with commercial-grade solutions.

Specialty Chemicals

RO Antiscalants

Our antiscalants effectively inhibit scale formation while dispersing colloidal particles, ensuring optimal membrane performance and longevity.

RO Cleaners

Our cleaners provide efficient and cost-effective removal of various foulants and scales from membranes, restoring their efficiency.

Cleaners for MF/UF Applications

Specially formulated for microfiltration and ultrafiltration, these cleaners are designed to remove common fouling such as organic fouling, metal fouling, and particulate fouling in a cost-effective manner.

Chlorine Scavengers

Chlorine in feedwater can cause irreversible damage to membranes, leading to premature failure. Our chlorine scavengers help protect membranes, extending their lifespan and reducing replacement costs.
