Water Treatment Chemicals

Speciality Chemicals for Every Water Treatment Process

Genoasis supplies a targeted portfolio of water treatment chemicals formulated specifically for reverse osmosis membranes, cooling water circuits, boiler systems, and industrial effluent treatment. All formulations are tested for compatibility with leading membrane brands and comply with relevant safety and environmental standards.

Our technical team provides dosing guidance, system audit, and chemical optimisation services to ensure maximum membrane life, minimum fouling, and compliant discharge. Products are available in 25 L containers, 200 L drums, and 1000 L IBC totes for continuous dosing systems across operations of any scale.

Product Range

Chemical Product Lines

Antiscalants

Phosphonate and polymer-based antiscalant formulations for preventing carbonate, sulphate, silica, and fluoride scale on RO membranes. Compatible with all thin-film composite membranes; dosed at 2–5 ppm for maximum protection.

Coagulants & Flocculants

Inorganic coagulants (ferric sulphate, alum, PAC) and organic flocculants (cationic and anionic polyacrylamides) for turbidity removal, TSS reduction, and sludge conditioning in clarifiers and DAF systems.

Biocides & Disinfectants

Non-oxidising biocides (isothiazolone, DBNPA, glutaraldehyde) for microbiological control in RO feed water, cooling towers, and storage tanks. Oxidising biocides including sodium hypochlorite and chlorine dioxide solutions also available.

Membrane Cleaners

Acid and caustic membrane cleaning formulations for removing carbonate and metal oxide scale, biofilm, and organic foulants from RO and NF membranes. Citric acid, EDTA, SDS-based, and alkaline enzyme cleaner options available.

pH Adjustment Chemicals

Technical-grade sulphuric acid, hydrochloric acid, caustic soda solution, and sodium bicarbonate for feed water pH correction ahead of RO systems and for conditioning effluent before discharge or biological treatment.

Oxygen Scavengers & Corrosion Inhibitors

Sodium bisulphite, DEHA, and hydrazine-free oxygen scavengers for boiler feed water treatment and corrosion control. Film-forming corrosion inhibitors for cooling water circuits protect metal surfaces and extend equipment life.

Why Choose Us

Key Features & Benefits

Effective at Low Dosages

High-activity formulations deliver superior performance at minimal treat rates, reducing chemical consumption costs and simplifying dosing system design.

Membrane Compatible

All RO chemicals are tested for compatibility with TFC, CA, and PVDF membranes from major manufacturers, ensuring no adverse effects on membrane integrity or performance.

Biodegradable Options

Environmentally responsible biodegradable antiscalant and biocide formulations are available for projects requiring minimal ecological impact and sustainable operation.

Technical Support Included

Our water treatment chemists provide dosing calculations, ROSA/Chem analysis, and on-site commissioning support to ensure optimal chemical programme performance.
