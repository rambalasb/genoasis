Product Range

Chemical Product Lines

Antiscalants

Phosphonate and polymer-based antiscalant formulations for preventing carbonate, sulphate, silica, and fluoride scale on RO membranes. Compatible with all thin-film composite membranes; dosed at 2–5 ppm for maximum protection.

Coagulants & Flocculants

Inorganic coagulants (ferric sulphate, alum, PAC) and organic flocculants (cationic and anionic polyacrylamides) for turbidity removal, TSS reduction, and sludge conditioning in clarifiers and DAF systems.

Biocides & Disinfectants

Non-oxidising biocides (isothiazolone, DBNPA, glutaraldehyde) for microbiological control in RO feed water, cooling towers, and storage tanks. Oxidising biocides including sodium hypochlorite and chlorine dioxide solutions also available.

Membrane Cleaners

Acid and caustic membrane cleaning formulations for removing carbonate and metal oxide scale, biofilm, and organic foulants from RO and NF membranes. Citric acid, EDTA, SDS-based, and alkaline enzyme cleaner options available.

pH Adjustment Chemicals

Technical-grade sulphuric acid, hydrochloric acid, caustic soda solution, and sodium bicarbonate for feed water pH correction ahead of RO systems and for conditioning effluent before discharge or biological treatment.

Oxygen Scavengers & Corrosion Inhibitors

Sodium bisulphite, DEHA, and hydrazine-free oxygen scavengers for boiler feed water treatment and corrosion control. Film-forming corrosion inhibitors for cooling water circuits protect metal surfaces and extend equipment life.

Common Questions

Water Treatment Chemicals FAQs

What water treatment chemicals does Genoasis supply?

We supply antiscalants (phosphonate and polymer-based), coagulants and flocculants (ferric sulphate, alum, PAC, polyacrylamide), non-oxidising biocides (isothiazolone, DBNPA, glutaraldehyde), membrane CIP cleaners (acid and alkaline), pH adjustment chemicals, and oxygen scavengers and corrosion inhibitors for boiler and cooling water systems.

Which antiscalant should I use for high-TDS brackish water in India?

For high-TDS borewell water in India (1,500–8,000 ppm TDS), we recommend a phosphonate or polymer antiscalant targeting carbonate, sulphate, and silica scale. Our technical team can run a scale prediction analysis from your water report and recommend the right product and dosing rate for your specific feed water chemistry and RO system recovery.

What dosing rate is required for RO antiscalants?

RO antiscalants are typically dosed at 2–5 ppm into the RO feed water. The exact dose depends on your feed water chemistry, system recovery rate, and scale-forming tendency. Our high-activity formulations deliver effective scale prevention at these low treat rates, keeping chemical running costs low.

Do you supply membrane cleaning (CIP) chemicals?

Yes. We supply both acid CIP formulations (citric acid and HCl blends, EDTA-based) for carbonate and metal oxide scale removal, and alkaline CIP cleaners (caustic soda, SDS-based enzyme formulations) for biofilm and organic foulant removal from RO membranes. Available in 25 L, 200 L drum, and 1,000 L IBC.
