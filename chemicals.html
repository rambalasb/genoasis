Specialty Chemicals & Polymers

RO Antiscalants

High-performance antiscalants designed to prevent scale formation in reverse osmosis systems, ensuring optimal membrane performance and extending system life.

  • Prevents calcium carbonate, calcium sulfate, and barium sulfate scaling
  • Compatible with all major RO membrane types
  • Reduces cleaning frequency and maintenance costs
  • Improves system recovery rates and water production
  • Long-lasting protection against scale formation
  • Effective in high-recovery RO systems
  • Compatible with various feedwater chemistries

RO Cleaners

  • High and low pH cleaning formulations
  • Effective against organic, inorganic, and biological fouling
  • Safe for all membrane types and materials
  • Restores membrane flux and rejection rates
  • Extends membrane life and reduces replacement costs
  • Removes iron, manganese, and silica deposits
  • Compatible with automated cleaning systems

Chlorine Scavengers

Chlorine in feedwater can cause irreversible damage to membranes, leading to premature failure. Our chlorine scavengers help protect membranes, extending their lifespan and reducing replacement costs.

  • Prevents irreversible membrane damage from chlorine
  • Extends membrane lifespan significantly
  • Reduces replacement costs and downtime
  • Compatible with all membrane types
  • Essential for feedwater pretreatment
  • Neutralizes free chlorine and chloramines
  • Maintains optimal membrane performance

CATIONIC POLYACRYLAMIDE (PAM)

Cationic PAM is a linear organic polymer, copolymerized with cationic monomers and acrylamide, offering excellent water solubility and used primarily as a flocculant in solid-liquid separation processes.

  • Linear organic polymer with excellent water solubility
  • Used as flocculant in solid-liquid separation processes
  • Effective in sewage treatment and paper manufacturing
  • Dry strength agent and retention aid in paper industry
  • Oil field applications for wastewater treatment
  • Anti-clay swelling and acidification applications
  • Reliable and efficient flocculation with minimal dosage
