Valves, Couplings & Fittings

A comprehensive range of PVC, stainless steel, and brass valves, high-pressure RO fittings, and quick-connect couplings engineered for reliable performance in water treatment systems and chemical handling applications.

Valves, Couplings & Fittings

Precision Flow Control for Every Water Treatment Application

Genoasis stocks a broad range of valves, couplings, and fittings covering every flow control and connection requirement from low-pressure pre-treatment pipework through to high-pressure RO and chemical dosing systems. Material options span UPVC, CPVC, polypropylene, stainless steel 316L, and brass to match the chemical compatibility, pressure, and temperature requirements of each application.

Our high-pressure RO fitting programme is especially comprehensive, stocking John Guest-style push-fit fittings, Parker-compatible compression fittings, and John Guest speedfit connectors in all standard sizes for RO skid fabrication and field service. Solenoid valves, pressure relief valves, and actuated ball valves with pneumatic and electric actuators are also available for automated system builds.

Product Range

Valve & Fitting Types

Ball Valves

Full-bore and reduced-bore ball valves in PVC, CPVC, PP, SS304, and SS316L for isolation and shut-off service. Available in ½" to 4" BSP/NPT and flanged ends with manual, actuated, and lockable handle options.

Butterfly Valves

Wafer and lug pattern butterfly valves in cast iron, ductile iron, and SS316 for large-diameter isolation and throttling duty from 2" to 24". Rubber-lined seats available in EPDM, NBR, and Viton for chemical compatibility.

Check Valves

Swing, dual plate, and spring-loaded piston check valves in PVC and stainless steel for preventing backflow in pump outlets, RO permeate lines, and chemical dosing systems. Silent-check variants available for pulsation-sensitive circuits.

Solenoid Valves

2/2 and 3/2 normally-closed and normally-open solenoid valves in brass, SS316, and PVC for automated flow control in RO flush systems, dosing circuits, backwash initiation, and process control applications. Available in 12V DC to 240V AC coil voltages.

High-Pressure RO Fittings

Stainless steel and nylon push-in and compression fittings rated to 150 psi for RO skid plumbing. Includes elbows, tees, reducers, and straight connectors in 3/8", ½", and ¾" tube OD for permeate, concentrate, and feed lines.

Quick-Connect Couplings & UPVC Fittings

Camlock couplings, sanitary tri-clamp fittings, and a full range of UPVC socket, threaded, and flanged fittings for RO pre-treatment systems and chemical handling pipework from ½" to 6" diameter.
