Valves, Pipes & Fittings for Water & Wastewater Treatment

Genoasis Technologies offers an extensive range of high-quality pipes, fittings, and valves designed to ensure seamless fluid management in water and wastewater systems. Our products guarantee durability, reliability, and superior performance in all phases of water treatment.

Pipes & Fittings

  • PVC and CPVC Pipe and Fittings: Durable solutions for water flow management
  • Customized Fittings: Tailored to meet the specific needs of each system

Valves

We supply a wide variety of metallic valves, which are engineered for performance in a range of industrial applications. Our valves include:

  • Ball Valves
  • Butterfly Valves
  • Globe Valves
  • Needle Valves
  • Gate Valves
  • Pressure Relief Valves
  • Non-Return / Check Valves

These valves are built for durability, efficiency, and long-term reliability, ensuring smooth operations and reliable performance.

Specialized Couplings: Victaulic Couplings

We are proud to offer Victaulic couplings, renowned for their high-performance sealing and ease of installation. These couplings are ideal for small to large diameter piping systems and are designed to provide a rigid joint with no expansion, contraction, or linear movement.

Key Features

  • Patented Angled Pad Design: Locks housing keys into the groove around the full circumference, creating a rigid connection.
  • Rigid Joint: No flexibility required, making them ideal for mechanical rooms, risers, and other areas where flexibility is not desired.
  • Compliance: Victaulic couplings meet ASME B31.1 Power Piping Code, ASME B31.9 Building Services Piping Code, and NFPA 13 Sprinkler Systems Code.
