UV Sterilizer Technology

Genoasis UV sterilizers use ultraviolet light to inactivate harmful microorganisms, including bacteria, viruses, and protozoa. The UV disinfection process is highly effective, chemical-free, and environmentally friendly, making it ideal for treating drinking water, wastewater, and process water.

Applications

  • Municipal water treatment
  • Food & beverage industry
  • Pharmaceutical water treatment
  • Wastewater treatment

Ozone Generator Systems

Our ozone generators offer an advanced oxidation process to purify water, eliminate odors, and destroy microorganisms. Ozone is a powerful oxidant that effectively treats both organic and inorganic contaminants in water without leaving harmful residues.

Applications

  • Drinking water purification
  • Industrial wastewater treatment
  • Aquaculture and pool water disinfection
  • Food and beverage industry
