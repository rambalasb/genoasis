Disinfection Products

Reliable Disinfection for Potable Water, Reuse, and Process Applications

Effective disinfection is critical to public health protection and process integrity in water treatment. Genoasis offers a comprehensive range of disinfection technologies to suit every application, budget, and regulatory requirement — from chemical-free UV and ozone systems through to economical chlorination and sodium hypochlorite dosing equipment.

All systems are supplied with automated control panels, flow-proportional dosing, residual monitoring capability, and comprehensive commissioning support. Our team assists with disinfection by-product (DBP) management and regulatory compliance for both drinking water and treated wastewater reuse applications.

Product Range

Disinfection Technologies

UV Disinfection Systems

Low-pressure and medium-pressure UV disinfection reactors from 20 to 10,000 L/h for chemical-free inactivation of bacteria, viruses, and Cryptosporidium. NSF 55 certified units with automated lamp intensity monitoring and sleeve cleaning.

Ozone Generators

Corona discharge ozone generators from 5 g/h to 1 kg/h output for advanced oxidation, microbiological control, taste and odour removal, and colour reduction in water and wastewater treatment. Complete with contact vessels and off-gas destructors.

Chlorination Equipment

Gas chlorinators, chlorine cylinder scales, and vacuum-operated chlorine dosing systems for municipal water treatment and large-scale disinfection. Includes safety equipment, leak detection, and emergency shut-off systems.

Sodium Hypochlorite Dosing Systems

Complete NaOCl dosing skids with peristaltic or diaphragm metering pumps, calibration cylinders, day tanks, and flow-proportional control for reliable automated chlorine residual maintenance in water distribution systems.

Chlorine Dioxide Systems

On-site chlorine dioxide generation systems for Legionella control, biofilm removal, and disinfection in cooling towers, hospital water systems, and process water circuits where chlorine residuals are undesirable.

Electrochlorination Systems

On-site sodium hypochlorite generation by electrolysis of salt solution. Eliminates hazardous chemical transport and storage while producing a consistent, low-concentration NaOCl solution for dosing into water systems.

Why Choose Us

Key Features & Benefits

Chemical-Free Options

UV and ozone disinfection systems provide highly effective treatment without adding chemicals, eliminating disinfection by-products and chemical handling risks.

Automated Control

PLC-based control panels with flow proportional dosing, UV intensity monitoring, and alarm outputs ensure consistent disinfection performance with minimal operator intervention.

High Pathogen Effectiveness

Our disinfection systems are validated against bacteria, viruses, Giardia, and Cryptosporidium, meeting WHO, IS, and international drinking water standards for microbial safety.

Scalable Systems

Disinfection solutions are available from small point-of-use units to large municipal-scale systems, with modular designs that can be expanded as treatment capacity grows.
