Filtration Gravel

We supply premium filtration sand and gravel, featuring high silica content (SiO2 ≥ 99%), which meets ANSI/AWWA B‑100‑09 standards. Our filtration sand is precisely graded for both domestic and industrial plants, while our gravel is highly spherical, ensuring smooth water flow and effective distribution in support beds. Anthracite

Anthracite is a key media for water filtration, offering multiple advantages such as:

Effective filtration for drinking and industrial water clarification
Higher service flow rates and longer filter runs with less head loss
Odor and color reduction
Removes residual hardness salts after clarification
Ideal for municipal potable water treatment; less backwashing

Activated Filter Media (AFM)

Activated Filter Media (AFM) is an advanced filtration medium produced from recycled glass. It undergoes a unique activation process to enhance its physical and catalytic properties, making it an excellent choice for high‑performance filtration. The activated glass has a mesoporous structure with an enormous catalytic surface area of 50,000 m² per m³, 300 times larger than traditional filter media.

AFM Sizes:

AFM‑00: 0.25 – 0.5 mm
AFM‑01: 0.5 – 1.0 mm
AFM‑02: 1.0 – 2.0 mm
AFM‑03: 2.0 – 4.0 mm

AFM provides exceptional bio‑resistance, high performance, and improved filtration efficiency.
