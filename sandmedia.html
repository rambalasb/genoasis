Ion Exchange Resins

We offer a wide variety of ion-exchange resins, which are used in a range of applications including water softening, purification, and the production of high-purity water. These resins are produced from an organic polymer substrate and come in different types, based on their functional groups:

  • Strongly Acidic Resins (e.g. sodium polystyrene sulfonate)
  • Strongly Basic Resins (e.g. trimethylammonium groups)
  • Weakly Acidic Resins (e.g. carboxylic acid groups)
  • Weakly Basic Resins (e.g. polyethylene amine)

Uses include:

  • Water softening
  • Water purification
  • High purity water production
  • Juice purification
  • Sugar manufacturing
  • Pharmaceuticals

Activated Carbon

We provide activated carbon solutions to municipalities and industries requiring water treatment to ensure safe drinking water. Our activated carbon effectively removes:

  • Taste and odor components (e.g., MIB and geosmin)
  • Pesticides and their metabolites
  • Organic matter (TOC) and disinfection by-products (DBPs)
  • Algae toxins (e.g., cyanotoxins)
  • Man-made chemicals (e.g., PFAS)
  • Volatile organic compounds (VOCs)
  • Endocrine disrupting compounds (EDCs)
  • Pharmaceuticals and personal care products (PPCPs)

All products comply with national regulations and certifications, ensuring high-quality performance in water purification.

Anthracite

Anthracite is a key media for water filtration, offering multiple advantages such as:

  • Effective Filtration: Ideal for water clarification in drinking and industrial water treatment.
  • Improved Flow Rates: Promotes higher service flow rates and longer filter runs with less head loss.
  • Odor and Color Reduction: Helps remove unwanted smells and colors from water.
  • Residual Hardness Removal: Effective in removing residual hardness salts after clarification.
  • Municipal Water Treatment: Ideal for municipal potable water treatment, requiring less backwashing and offering better results.
