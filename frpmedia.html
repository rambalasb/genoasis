RO Pressure Vessels & FRP Filters

Engineered fibre-reinforced polymer vessels for reverse osmosis membrane housing and multi-media pre-treatment filtration across industrial and commercial water systems.

Pressure Vessels & FRP Filters

High-Performance FRP Vessels for Every RO Application

Our fibre-reinforced polymer (FRP) pressure vessels are designed to house standard 4", 8", and 16" diameter RO membrane elements, delivering outstanding structural integrity at operating pressures up to 600 psi. The smooth internal liner prevents biofilm accumulation and provides years of trouble-free performance in seawater, brackish water, and high-TDS applications.

In addition to membrane housings, we supply FRP multi-media and sand filter vessels for pre-treatment stages. Available in a wide range of diameters and with top or side-mount port configurations, these vessels are the backbone of reliable RO and filtration systems worldwide.

Product Range

Vessels & Filter Products

4" RO Pressure Vessels

Standard 4" diameter vessels accommodating 2- to 7-element configurations. Ideal for small to medium commercial RO systems, rated up to 300 psi operating pressure with end-port or side-port options.

8" RO Pressure Vessels

Industry-standard 8" diameter vessels for large industrial RO plants. Typically houses 6 membrane elements in series, engineered for pressures up to 600 psi in seawater desalination duty.

16" RO Pressure Vessels

Large-diameter 16" vessels designed for high-capacity industrial and municipal RO plants. Constructed from premium-grade FRP with internal thermoplastic liner for chemical resistance and longevity.

Composite Vessels

Carbon-fibre composite pressure vessels offering reduced weight with superior pressure ratings. Suitable for skid-mounted systems and mobile water treatment units where weight is a critical factor.

FRP Multi-media Filters

Large-diameter FRP filter vessels for multi-media, sand, carbon, and softener applications. Available in manual or automatic backwash configurations, suitable for flow rates from 1 to over 200 m³/h.

FRP Sand Filters

Purpose-built FRP vessels for pressure sand filtration of turbid water. Corrosion-proof construction handles aggressive raw-water chemistries that would damage conventional steel tanks.

Common Questions

FRP Pressure Vessel FAQs

What FRP pressure vessel sizes does Genoasis supply?

We supply 4" RO vessels (for 4"×40" elements), 8" RO vessels (for 8"×40" elements), 16" large-diameter composite vessels for high-capacity industrial systems, and FRP multi-media and sand filter vessels. Composite vessels are also available for high-pressure SWRO service up to 600 PSI.

Are the pressure vessels NSF certified?

Yes. Our FRP pressure vessels are NSF/ANSI 61 certified for potable water contact, and manufactured to ASME and ISO standards. Full certification documentation, test certificates, and data sheets are provided with each supply.

What is the maximum operating pressure for FRP RO vessels?

Standard FRP vessels are rated to 300 PSI (21 bar) — sufficient for most BWRO applications. For SWRO high-pressure service, we supply composite vessels rated to 600 PSI (41 bar). Contact us with your operating pressure and membrane configuration for the right vessel recommendation.

Can FRP vessels be used for seawater RO desalination in UAE and Saudi Arabia?

Yes. FRP and composite pressure vessels are the standard choice for SWRO desalination plants across UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, and Oman. FRP is inherently corrosion-resistant to chloride — making it far superior to metallic alternatives in the Gulf's high-temperature, high-chloride seawater environment.
