RO Pressure Vessels & FRP Media Filter Solutions

We are a leading supplier and trader of premium RO pressure vessels and FRP media filters, designed to meet the demands of various commercial and industrial water treatment applications. Our solutions are engineered for cost-effective, continuous, and long-term use, offering optimal performance in a variety of operating conditions. At Genoasis Technologies, we offer a wide range of RO pressure vessels and FRP media filters designed to meet your specific water treatment needs. Whether you are handling municipal, industrial, or residential water treatment, our solutions are engineered to provide the highest level of performance and durability.

RO Membrane Housings

Our RO membrane housings are the perfect solution for a wide range of applications, including:

  • Municipal water treatment
  • Power generation
  • Food and beverage processing
  • Ultra-pure water production
  • Oil & Gas

These membrane housings are constructed from fibre-reinforced epoxy/glass composites and can accommodate up to 8 membrane elements. They are designed to handle a wide range of operating pressures, including 150 psi, 300 psi, 450 psi, 600 psi, 1000 psi, and 1200 psi. Our RO vessels are highly compatible for brackish, high brackish, and seawater applications.

Membrane housings with 8" and 4" diameters are the most commonly used worldwide across a variety of water treatment projects.

8-Inch Side Entry Pressure Vessel

Our 8-inch side entry membrane housings are available in a wide operating pressure range from 150 to 1200 psi. The multi-port design allows vessel-to-vessel connection, minimizing the need for costly manifolds. Patented OCTA technology uses side ports mounted on flat sealing surfaces for easy servicing.

  • 80S Series: Available with side-port options of 1.5, 2.0, and 2.5-inch IPS grooved.
  • 80H Series: Available with side-port options of 3.0-inch IPS grooved.
  • 80U Series: Available with side-port options of 4.0-inch IPS grooved.

Our 8-inch end entry membrane housings are also available in operating pressures of 300, 450, 600, 1000, and 1200 psi.

4-Inch Side Entry Pressure Vessel

Our 4-inch side entry membrane housings are available in operating pressures of 300 psi, 450 psi, and 600 psi. The multi-port feature in 4-inch membrane housings enables vessel-to-vessel connections, reducing the need for costly manifolds. Additionally, 4-inch end-entry membrane housings are available in operating pressures of 300, 600, and 1000 psi, designed to accommodate any standard 40-inch-long membrane.
