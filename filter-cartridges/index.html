Filter Cartridge & Housing Types

String Wound PP Cartridges

Depth filtration string wound polypropylene cartridges for high dirt-holding capacity on sediment and turbidity. Available 1-50 micron in 10", 20", 30" and 40" lengths for RO pre-filtration.

Melt Blown PP Cartridges

Melt blown (spun) polypropylene cartridges with graded density for consistent sediment removal. The most popular 5 micron RO pre-filter, in 10", 20", 30" and 40" lengths.

Pleated Filter Cartridges

Large surface-area pleated polyester/PP cartridges for high flow and long life. Washable and reusable grades available in 1-20 micron ratings for fine sediment and colloid removal.

Carbon Block Cartridges

Activated carbon block cartridges that remove chlorine, taste, odour and organics to protect thin-film RO membranes. Available in 5 and 10 micron ratings and 10", 20" lengths.

Filter Housings (PP & SS)

Polypropylene housings in standard and Big Blue sizes, plus stainless steel multi-cartridge housings for high-flow industrial duty. Matched to your cartridge length, count and connection size.

FAQs

Filter Cartridges - Common Questions

Technical answers for water treatment engineers and buyers in India, the UAE and the GCC.

Which filter cartridge types does Genoasis supply?

We supply string wound polypropylene cartridges, melt blown PP (spun) cartridges, pleated polyester/PP cartridges and activated carbon block cartridges. All are available in 1, 5, 10, 20 and 50 micron ratings and standard 10", 20", 30" and 40" lengths to fit standard filter housings.

What micron rating should I use for RO pre-filtration?

A 5 micron melt blown or string wound polypropylene cartridge is the most common choice for RO pre-filtration, protecting the high-pressure pump and membranes from sediment. Where feed water carries fine colloids we recommend a 5 micron pleated cartridge, and carbon block cartridges are added upstream to remove chlorine that can oxidise thin-film RO membranes.

Do you also supply filter housings for the cartridges?

Yes. We supply polypropylene housings, including standard and Big Blue sizes, for 10", 20" and jumbo cartridges, and stainless steel multi-cartridge housings for higher flow and pressure industrial duty. We match the housing, cartridge count and connection size to your required flow rate.

Do you supply filter cartridges to the UAE, Saudi Arabia and Africa?

Yes. We supply filter cartridges and housings across the Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait, Bahrain), India, and Africa (Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Ghana, Tanzania, Ethiopia). We handle export documentation and shipping, and respond to enquiries within 1 hour.
