Nurturing Life, Innovating Water:
Water Bird, Transforming Tomorrow

Waterbird Water Treatment Chemicals L.L.C was established in late 1980s, envisioning the need for conservation of WATER, a scarce but unique element that has contributed to the development of all life forms on our planet. Water also means challenge for us as we see an enormous potential in every aspect of water and waste water treatment. Our comprehensive expertise in water treatment applications and processes have laid the path for a future of water treatment thanks to our environmentally friendly, innovative, and industry-specific solutions. Hygienic clean water is a result of complex treatment processes. We are proficient in all relevant and innovative water treatment technologies, irrespective of the methodology.

Water Treatment
Solution

Water is an essential nutrient. Not only for human consumption, for industrial processes too. Water treatment is used to optimize most water-based industrial processes, such as: heating, cooling, processing, cleaning, and rinsing, so that operating costs and risks are reduced.

Filtration and Reverse Osmosis Division

Water Bird: Elevating Water Quality. Explore our comprehensive range of filtration solutionsfrom Sand and Multi-Media Filters to Self-Cleaning and Disc Filters. We address suspended solids, turbidity, hardness, and various contaminants, safeguarding water supplies and wastewater for a sustainable future.

Waste Water Treatment Division

We are specialized in all kinds of Domestic and Industrial waste water treatment and re-use systems. Our specialized treatment system includes Design, Engineering, Supply, Installation, Testing/Commissioning and handover

Specialty Chemicals
Division

Water Bird's Specialty Chemicals: Tailored Solutions for Water Excellence. From Coagulation to Odor Control, our diverse range of precise formulations addresses specific system needs, ensuring optimal water and wastewater treatment.

New HVAC Systems
