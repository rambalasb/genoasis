Power & Control for Your Entire Plant

Genoasis supplies the complete electrical backbone of a water treatment plant - from premium-efficiency motors and variable frequency drives that run your pumps and blowers, to control and motor control centre (MCC) panels that bring it all together under one safe, certified system.

Common Questions

Electrical & Industrial Supply FAQs

What electrical products does Genoasis supply for water treatment plants?

We supply electric motors (IE2/IE3 TEFC induction motors), variable frequency drives (VFDs), control and MCC panels, switchgear, contactors, overload relays, soft starters, cables, cable glands, and general industrial supplies for pump, blower, dosing, and plant automation loads across water and wastewater treatment installations.

Do you build custom control and MCC panels?

Yes. We supply and build custom control panels, motor control centres (MCCs), and PLC/SCADA-ready automation panels to your single-line diagram and IP rating requirements. Panels are assembled with branded components and can be supplied with type-test certification and wiring documentation.

Are the motors and drives energy efficient?

Yes. We supply IE3 premium-efficiency motors and variable frequency drives that reduce energy consumption on pumps, blowers, and fans. VFD control on pumping and aeration loads typically delivers significant energy savings versus fixed-speed operation, lowering the lifetime operating cost of the plant.

Can you supply electrical and industrial items to the Middle East and Africa?

Yes. Genoasis supplies electrical and general industrial items across the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, and Bahrain, as well as Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Ghana, Tanzania, and Ethiopia. We assist with component selection, voltage/frequency specification (220-415V, 50/60Hz), and full export documentation for customs clearance.

How do I request a quote for electrical or industrial supplies?

Send us your bill of materials, load list, or single-line diagram and we will respond within 1 hour with pricing and availability. Email info@genoasis.com, call +91 892 588 3227 (India) or +971 50 497 3835 (UAE), or use our contact form. For panels, share the kW ratings, supply voltage, IP rating, and any branded component preferences.
