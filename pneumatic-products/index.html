Product Range

Pneumatic Component Types

ADN Compact Cylinders

ISO 21287-compliant compact pneumatic cylinders in bore sizes 12–100 mm and strokes from 5–200 mm. Short installation length, adjustable cushioning, and integrated magnetic piston for proximity sensor compatibility.

DFM Guided Actuators

Twin-rod guided drives with high torque and lateral force resistance for precise linear positioning in assembly, pressing, and pick-and-place applications. Available in bore sizes 12–63 mm with adjustable end-position cushioning.

DSNU Round Cylinders

ISO 6432 compliant round-body cylinders in 8–32 mm bore for light-duty clamping, ejecting, and locking applications. Compact round barrel design with magnetic piston; suitable for both horizontal and vertical mounting.

Directional Control Valves

Solenoid-operated 5/2, 5/3, and 3/2 directional control valves in sub-base and inline mounting for single and double-acting cylinder control. Available in manual override, monostable, and bistable configurations from G⅛ to G½.

Air Preparation Units (FRL)

Filter-regulator-lubricator (FRL) combinations, compressed air filters with 5–40 micron element options, pressure regulators with gauge, and micro-fog lubricators for maintaining clean, dry, and correctly pressurised instrument air supplies.

Rotary Actuators & Grippers

Rack-and-pinion rotary actuators for 90° and 180° valve operation, plus parallel and angular pneumatic grippers for automated handling and assembly systems. Suitable for integration with solenoid valve islands and PLC control.

Key Features & Benefits

ISO Standard Dimensions

All cylinders and valves conform to ISO mounting and port dimensions, ensuring direct interchangeability with existing equipment and simplifying spare parts management.

Long Service Life

Hardened piston rods, wear-resistant seals, and precision-machined bores deliver consistent performance over tens of millions of operating cycles in demanding industrial environments.

High Precision

Tight manufacturing tolerances and integrated cushioning provide smooth, precise positioning and controlled deceleration, reducing mechanical shock and extending equipment life.

Sensor Compatible

Magnetic pistons and T-slot cylinder profiles accommodate all standard proximity sensor types, enabling position feedback without modification to the cylinder body.
