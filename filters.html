Complete range of depth and surface filtration cartridges paired with polypropylene, stainless steel, and multi-bag housings for pre-treatment, process water, and point-of-use applications.

Filter Cartridges & Housings

Reliable Particulate Removal Across All Micron Ratings

Our cartridge filter programme covers every stage of water treatment filtration from coarse 100-micron pre-filtration through to absolute 0.2-micron sterilising-grade polishing. All cartridge types are manufactured from food-grade polypropylene, ensuring compliance with drinking water and food & beverage applications.

Compatible housings range from single-cartridge residential units to multi-cartridge stainless steel vessels handling hundreds of cubic metres per hour. We supply standard 10", 20", 30", and 40" cartridges in all major formats, ensuring drop-in replacement compatibility with existing systems regardless of the original manufacturer.

Product Range

Cartridge Types & Housings

String Wound Polypropylene Cartridges

Depth-filtration cartridges with graded density wound construction for high dirt-holding capacity. Available in 1–100 micron ratings; economical and widely used in RO pre-treatment and general industrial filtration.

Melt Blown Polypropylene Cartridges

Thermally bonded melt-blown cartridges offering consistent micron ratings from 0.5 to 50 microns. No binder or adhesive — 100% polypropylene construction ensures chemical compatibility and clean filtration.

Pleated Filter Cartridges

High surface area pleated membrane cartridges in PP, PES, PVDF, and nylon for low pressure drop and long service life. Available in absolute micron ratings from 0.2 to 10 microns for critical applications.

Carbon Block Cartridges

Extruded activated carbon block cartridges for simultaneous particulate filtration and chlorine, taste, and odour removal. Essential for protecting TFC RO membranes from chlorine oxidation damage.

Polypropylene Filter Housings

Single and multi-cartridge transparent and opaque PP housings in 10" and 20" sizes. Pressure-rated to 125 psi with NSF-compliant O-ring seals; ideal for residential, commercial, and light industrial service.

Stainless Steel Multi-Cartridge Housings

SS304 and SS316L housings accommodating 1 to 48 cartridges for high-flow industrial applications. Rated up to 150 psi and 90°C for hot water and steam-sanitisable process water systems.

Why Choose Us

Key Features & Benefits

Wide Micron Ratings

From 100-micron nominal to 0.2-micron absolute, our cartridge range addresses every particulate challenge from coarse pre-filtration to sterilising-grade polishing.

Food-Grade Materials

All cartridges manufactured from virgin, FDA-compliant polypropylene with no binders or extractable substances, ensuring safe use in drinking water and food processing.

High Dirt-Holding Capacity

Graded-density depth construction maximises dirt-holding capacity and service intervals, reducing cartridge consumption and maintenance frequency.

Universal Compatibility

Standard 222 and 226 end-cap configurations and DOE (double open end) designs ensure compatibility with all major housing brands and OEM filtration equipment.
