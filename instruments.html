Instruments & Lab Apparatus

Precise water quality instruments and laboratory apparatus for on-line monitoring, field testing, and accredited laboratory analysis in water treatment, industrial, and research applications.

Instruments & Lab Apparatus

Accurate Measurement for Reliable Water Treatment Operations

Reliable water quality measurement is the foundation of efficient and compliant water treatment. Genoasis supplies a carefully selected range of portable, bench-top, and panel-mounted instruments covering all critical water quality parameters including pH, ORP, TDS/EC, turbidity, dissolved oxygen, chlorine, and flow rate.

Our laboratory apparatus programme complements the instrument range with high-quality borosilicate glassware, titration equipment, test kits, and analytical chemicals for both field and accredited laboratory water testing. All instruments are supplied with calibration standards, electrodes, and comprehensive documentation.

Product Range

Instruments & Apparatus

TDS & EC Meters

Portable and bench-top TDS and electrical conductivity meters with automatic temperature compensation (ATC). Measurement ranges from 0–9999 µS/cm to 0–200 mS/cm for permeate quality checking and source water assessment.

pH Meters & Electrodes

Laboratory, portable, and inline pH meters with replaceable glass combination electrodes. Resolution of 0.01 pH, automatic buffer recognition, and GLP data storage for HACCP and quality management systems.

Chlorine Analysers

Colorimetric and amperometric chlorine analysers for free and total chlorine measurement in drinking water, process water, and wastewater. DPD and FACTS reagent-based photometric test kits also stocked.

Turbidity Meters

Nephelometric turbidity meters (NTU) for online and portable water clarity measurement. EPA 180.1 and ISO 7027 compliant units covering 0.01–1000 NTU, essential for monitoring filter and clarifier performance.

Flow Meters

Electromagnetic, ultrasonic, and rotameter flow meters for water treatment pipework from 15 mm to 300 mm diameter. Inline and clamp-on ultrasonic options for non-invasive measurement on existing pipework.

Lab Glassware & Apparatus

Borosilicate glass beakers, graduated cylinders, burettes, pipettes, Erlenmeyer flasks, and jar test equipment for water quality analysis. Includes test kits for hardness, alkalinity, iron, silica, and other key parameters.

Why Choose Us

Key Features & Benefits

High Accuracy

Instruments are selected for their measurement accuracy, long-term stability, and minimal drift, ensuring reliable readings across extended operational periods.

Easy Calibration

One- and two-point calibration routines with automatic buffer recognition and calibration reminders simplify routine maintenance and ensure measurement traceability.

Portable & Bench-Top Options

Both field-portable (IP67-rated) and bench-top laboratory models are available, giving flexibility for on-site monitoring, laboratory analysis, and process control applications.

Data Logging & Connectivity

Many instruments feature built-in data logging, USB transfer, and RS485/4–20 mA outputs for integration with SCADA systems and automated process control panels.

FAQs

Water Quality Instruments — Common Questions

Technical guidance for water treatment plant operators in India, UAE, Saudi Arabia, and the GCC.

What water quality instruments does Genoasis supply?

Genoasis supplies portable and online TDS/conductivity meters, pH meters, ORP meters, dissolved oxygen (DO) meters, free and total chlorine analysers, turbidity meters (NTU), flow meters (electromagnetic, ultrasonic, rotameter), pressure gauges, and laboratory glassware and test kits for water quality analysis. Both portable field instruments and panel-mounted online monitors are available.

Do you supply online TDS monitors for RO permeate quality monitoring?

Yes. We supply inline and panel-mounted online TDS/conductivity monitors that provide continuous permeate quality measurement for RO systems. These are essential for detecting membrane failure or bypass, and for maintaining water quality records for regulatory compliance. Controller options with alarm outputs for automated system shutdown on quality breach are also available.

Do you supply chlorine analysers for drinking water treatment plants in India and UAE?

Yes. We supply portable and online free/total chlorine analysers for post-disinfection monitoring in drinking water treatment plants, swimming pools, and food processing facilities across India and the Middle East. Available in amperometric (electrochemical) and colorimetric (DPD) measurement technologies.

What flow meters do you supply for water treatment plants?

We supply variable-area rotameters for lower-cost flow indication, electromagnetic flow meters for conductive liquids (water, wastewater, chemical solutions), and ultrasonic clamp-on flow meters for non-intrusive measurement on existing pipework. Sizes from 15mm (½") to 300mm (12") and larger are available to suit all water treatment plant flow rates.
