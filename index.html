Innovative Water Treatment Solutions for Industrial Excellence

Genoasis is a leading provider of industrial water treatment solutions across India and the UAE. Our comprehensive range of products includes advanced FRP filters, RO vessels, membrane technologies, industrial pumps, and specialized water treatment chemicals. With offices in Chennai and Dubai, we serve diverse industries including power plants, oil & gas, food processing, pharmaceuticals, and municipal water systems.

Comprehensive Water Treatment Products

Our product portfolio encompasses all aspects of water treatment infrastructure. We supply high-performance RO pressure vessels constructed from fiber-reinforced plastic (FRP) for maximum durability and corrosion resistance. Our membrane products include reverse osmosis, ultrafiltration, and nanofiltration membranes from leading manufacturers. We also provide a complete range of water treatment pumps including centrifugal, submersible, and metering pumps designed for demanding industrial applications.

Advanced Technologies and Solutions

Genoasis specializes in cutting-edge water treatment technologies including atmospheric water generators, nanobubble systems, and advanced filtration media. Our chemical solutions portfolio includes coagulants, flocculants, biocides, scale inhibitors, and pH adjusters. We also supply essential equipment such as valves, couplings, fittings, filter cartridges, sand media, ion exchange resins, and instrumentation for complete water treatment facilities.

Industrial Expertise and Service

With extensive experience in water treatment for power generation, petrochemical, food & beverage, and wastewater treatment applications, Genoasis provides technical expertise and reliable support. Our team offers comprehensive project management, installation guidance, and ongoing maintenance support. We partner with leading manufacturers including Festo for industrial automation solutions and provide dunnage air bags for safe transportation of equipment. Learn about modern water treatment technologies from Water Online and industrial filtration standards from ANSI.
