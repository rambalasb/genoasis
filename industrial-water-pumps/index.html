Industrial Water Pumps

RO Feed, Process, Dewatering & Sea Water RO Pumps

Genoasis is a trusted supplier of industrial water and process pumps for water treatment, RO high-pressure feed, dewatering and desalination. Our range covers horizontal centrifugal (end-suction) pumps, vertical multistage high-pressure pumps for RO feed and boosting, submersible drainage and sewage pumps, borewell submersible pumps, air-operated double diaphragm (AODD) pumps for chemical and abrasive fluids, progressive cavity pumps for sludge, and sea water FRP & super duplex stainless steel pumps for SWRO desalination high-pressure duty.

Product Range

Industrial Water Pump Types

Horizontal Centrifugal Pumps

End-suction centrifugal pumps for clean water transfer, RO pre-treatment and general process duty. Cast iron, bronze and stainless steel construction for a wide range of flows and heads.

Vertical Multistage Pumps

High-pressure vertical multistage pumps for RO feed and boosting. Stainless steel construction delivers the pressure and flow needed to drive water through brackish water RO membranes.

Submersible Pumps

Submersible drainage and sewage pumps for dewatering, ETP/STP sumps and effluent transfer. Robust impellers handle solids-laden water and wastewater reliably.

Borewell Pumps

Borewell submersible pumps for deep-well water supply, irrigation and RO raw-water abstraction. Stainless steel stages for long life in high-head borewell applications.

AODD Pumps

Air-operated double diaphragm (AODD) pumps for chemical, abrasive and viscous fluids. Seal-less, self-priming and run-dry safe - ideal for chemical dosing and transfer duty.

Sea Water FRP & Super Duplex Pumps

Sea water FRP and super duplex stainless steel pumps for SWRO desalination high-pressure duty. Corrosion-resistant construction for reliable service on high-salinity seawater feed.

FAQs

Industrial Water Pumps - Common Questions

Technical answers for plant engineers and buyers in India, the UAE and the GCC.

Which industrial water pumps does Genoasis supply?

We supply horizontal centrifugal (end-suction) pumps, vertical multistage high-pressure pumps for RO feed and boosting, submersible drainage and sewage pumps, borewell submersible pumps, air-operated double diaphragm (AODD) pumps for chemical and abrasive fluids, progressive cavity pumps for sludge, and sea water FRP and super duplex stainless steel pumps for SWRO desalination high-pressure duty.

Which pump is used for RO high-pressure feed and SWRO desalination?

Vertical multistage pumps are used for brackish water RO high-pressure feed and boosting, delivering the pressure and flow required to drive water through the membranes. For seawater reverse osmosis (SWRO) desalination, we supply sea water FRP and super duplex stainless steel pumps engineered for high-pressure, corrosion-resistant duty on high-salinity feed.

Can you help select the right pump for my application?

Yes. Send us your required flow rate, head or discharge pressure, fluid type and temperature, and installation details. Our engineers recommend the correct pump type, material of construction and motor rating, and quote it together with your RO membranes, pressure vessels and chemicals in a single package.

Do you supply industrial water pumps to the UAE, Saudi Arabia and Africa?

Yes. We supply industrial water pumps across the Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait, Bahrain), India, and Africa (Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Ghana, Tanzania, Ethiopia). We handle export documentation and shipping, and respond to enquiries within 1 hour.
