Who We Are

Industrial water treatment specialists

Founded in 2020, Genoasis Water Technologies is a trusted supplier of industrial water treatment solutions, headquartered in Chennai, India with operations across the Middle East. We specialize in sourcing and supplying premium equipment, chemicals, and components from globally certified manufacturers.

Our team combines deep technical expertise with a customer-first approach — helping industries across Oil & Gas, Power, Food & Beverage, Desalination, and more achieve efficient, reliable, and sustainable water treatment outcomes.
