RO Antiscalants & Scale Inhibitors

Phosphonate & Polymer Antiscalants for RO Membranes

Genoasis is a trusted supplier of high-activity liquid RO antiscalants that keep reverse osmosis membranes clean and running at design flux. Our phosphonate-based and polymer-based concentrates inhibit carbonate, sulphate and silica scale, allowing higher system recovery and longer intervals between membrane cleanings. Grades are available for brackish water, high-TDS and high-silica feed, and seawater (SWRO).

Every antiscalant is matched to your application: send us your feed water analysis and target recovery, and our engineers run scale-prediction software to identify the limiting scale, recommend the correct antiscalant grade and confirm the optimum dose - typically 2-5 ppm into the RO feed. We also supply NSF/ANSI 60 certified grades for potable water plants, and provide MSDS and COA with every shipment.

RO antiscalants are supplied across India (Chennai, Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad), the Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait, Bahrain), and Africa (Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Ghana) in 25 L cans, 200 L drums and 1000 L IBCs. We respond to every enquiry within 1 hour.

Product Range

RO Antiscalant Grades

Phosphonate Antiscalants

Broad-spectrum phosphonate concentrates that control calcium carbonate, calcium and barium sulphate scale on brackish and high-hardness feed. Effective at low 2-4 ppm dosing for general-purpose RO duty.

Polymer Antiscalants

Polymer-based dispersant antiscalants that suspend colloidal foulants and inhibit metal-oxide and sulphate scale. Ideal for high-recovery RO and surface water where iron and colloids are present.

High-Silica Antiscalants

Specialised grades that keep reactive silica in solution well above its normal solubility limit, enabling high-recovery RO on high-silica borewell and geothermal feed without silica scaling.

Seawater (SWRO) Antiscalants

High-pressure seawater grades formulated for SWRO desalination, controlling carbonate and sulphate scale in high-TDS feed up to 50,000 ppm across the GCC and coastal Africa.

FAQs

RO Antiscalants - Common Questions

Technical answers for RO plant engineers and buyers in India, the UAE and the GCC.

What types of RO antiscalant does Genoasis supply?

We supply phosphonate-based and polymer-based high-activity liquid antiscalants for reverse osmosis. Grades are available for brackish water RO, high-TDS and high-silica feed, and seawater (SWRO), including NSF/ANSI 60 certified antiscalants for potable water applications. All grades are supplied with MSDS and COA.

What is the typical RO antiscalant dosing rate?

Typical RO antiscalant dosing is 2 to 5 ppm into the RO feed, injected upstream of the cartridge filters and high-pressure pump. The exact dose depends on feed water chemistry and system recovery; our engineers confirm it using scale-prediction software from your feed water analysis.

How do you select the right antiscalant for my feed water?

Send us your feed water analysis (TDS, hardness, silica, sulphates, temperature, pH) and target recovery. We run scale-prediction software to identify the limiting scale (carbonate, sulphate or silica), recommend the correct antiscalant grade and dose, and confirm no risk of precipitation at your operating recovery.

Do you supply RO antiscalants to the UAE, Saudi Arabia and Africa?

Yes. We supply RO antiscalants across the Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait, Bahrain), India, and Africa (Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Ghana, Tanzania, Ethiopia) in 25 L cans, 200 L drums and 1000 L IBCs. We handle export documentation and shipping, provide MSDS and COA, and respond to enquiries within 1 hour.
