Industrial Pumps

A comprehensive range of energy-efficient centrifugal, submersible, AODD, progressive cavity, and speciality seawater pumps for water treatment, irrigation, and industrial fluid handling.

Pumps

The Right Pump for Every Water Treatment Stage

Genoasis offers a carefully curated selection of industrial pumps engineered for water supply, RO feed, high-pressure service, seawater intake, chemical dosing, and sludge handling. Our portfolio spans horizontally mounted end-suction centrifugal units through to corrosion-proof FRP sea water pumps and duplex stainless steel models built for the harshest saline environments.

Each pump series is selected for hydraulic efficiency, reliability, and ease of maintenance. We assist with duty-point selection, material specification (standard cast iron, stainless steel 316L, FRP, super duplex), and after-sales support including spare parts and field service across India and the Middle East.

Product Range

Pump Types & Series

Horizontal Centrifugal Pumps

Single and multi-stage horizontal end-suction pumps for general water transfer, RO feed, and pressure boosting duties. Available in cast iron, SS304, and SS316L with IE3 motor options for energy compliance.

Vertical Multistage Pumps

In-line vertical multistage pumps delivering high heads in compact footprints. Ideal for RO high-pressure feed, booster stations, and pressure-boosting systems in commercial buildings and treatment plants.

Submersible Pumps

Stainless steel submersible pumps for sump, tank, and open-channel water transfer applications. Hermetically sealed motors rated for continuous submerged operation with integrated thermal protection.

Borewell Pumps

4" and 6" borewell submersible pumps for groundwater extraction in agricultural, municipal, and industrial applications. Stainless steel hydraulics ensure long service life in abrasive and sandy well conditions.

Air-Operated Double Diaphragm (AODD) Pumps

Pneumatically powered AODD pumps for chemical dosing, slurry transfer, and hazardous fluid handling. Self-priming and dry-run safe; available in polypropylene, PVDF, stainless steel, and aluminium body materials.

Progressive Cavity Pumps

Positive-displacement progressive cavity pumps for viscous, shear-sensitive, and abrasive media including sludge, polymer solutions, and chemical slurries. Low pulsation output with smooth, predictable flow metering.

Sea Water FRP Pumps

Fibre-reinforced polymer horizontal centrifugal pumps engineered specifically for seawater and aggressive brine service. Fully corrosion-proof wetted parts delivering years of reliable operation in coastal and offshore applications.

Sea Water Super Duplex Pumps

Super duplex stainless steel (UNS S32750) pumps for high-flow seawater intake, SWRO feed, and brine discharge applications. Superior chloride resistance and mechanical strength for the most demanding marine environments.

Why Choose Us

Key Features & Benefits

Energy Efficient

IE3 premium-efficiency motors and hydraulically optimised impellers reduce energy consumption, lowering operating costs across the pump's full service life.

Wide Flow Range

Our pump range covers flow rates from a few litres per minute up to thousands of cubic metres per hour, ensuring the right solution for every project scale.

Corrosion Resistant Options

Material options including SS316L, super duplex, FRP, and PVDF ensure optimal chemical and corrosion resistance for seawater, brine, and aggressive chemical service.

Easy Maintenance

Back pull-out design and standardised mechanical seal arrangements allow in-situ maintenance without disturbing pipe connections, minimising plant downtime.
