Pumps

Advanced Pumping Solutions for Every Industry

Comprehensive range of high-quality pumps designed for diverse applications including water treatment, chemical processing, industrial operations, and specialized environments.

End Suction Pumps

Our End Suction Pumps are ideal for applications requiring solid handling or use in chemical and food processing industries. With minimal moving parts and no valves, these pumps offer a compact solution that is easy to handle and operate. Key features include energy efficiency, easy maintenance, and long service life. These pumps are designed for high efficiency and low hydraulic thrust, making them versatile and reliable for both corrosive and non-corrosive liquids.

Horizontal Centrifugal Pumps

We supply a range of high-quality horizontal centrifugal pumps, including both horizontal multistage and single-stage models. These pumps are designed using the latest technology to meet the diverse needs of industries such as chemical, water and wastewater, sugar, steel, and power stations. They are also used for pumping hydrocarbons and in irrigation and water desalination plants.

Specifications:

Maximum Capacity: 1000 m³/hr
Maximum Head: 140 m
Temperature: Up to 160°C
Working Pressure: 14 kg/cm²

Vertical Multistage Pumps

Our Vertical Multistage Pumps are reliable, technologically advanced solutions for various applications such as boiler feed systems, pressure boosting, irrigation, and water treatment. These pumps are designed for high pressure and high-temperature fluids and are equipped with IE3 standard motors for energy efficiency. They come in several configurations to meet the specific flow rate and pressure needs of your operation.

Specifications:

Delivery: Up to 400 m³/h
Head: Up to 330 m
Power: From 0.37 kW to 300 kW
Maximum Pressure: 60 Bar
Temperature Range: From -30°C to 180°C

Sea Water FRP Pumps

Our Sea Water FRP Pumps are constructed using high-quality fiberglass reinforced plastic (FRP), making them ideal for handling seawater and aggressive chemicals. These pumps offer excellent resistance to corrosion and abrasion, providing a cost-effective alternative to metal alloy or plastic-lined pumps in seawater and other harsh environments.

Available Configurations:

Close Coupled Pump
Long Coupled Pump
Belt Driven Pump
Chemical Road Tanker Pump
Vertical Spindle Pump

Sea Water Super Duplex Pumps

Made from duplex steel, these pumps are ideal for applications involving seawater, such as desalination plants and offshore operations. Known for their high resistance to corrosion, abrasion, and stress corrosion cracking, these pumps are highly efficient and durable, providing reliable service in challenging environments.

Key Features:

High efficiency (up to 87%)
Super duplex construction (Zeron-100)
High inlet pressure options (up to 83 bar)
Self-lubricated for smooth operation

Submersible Pumps

Submersible Pumps are widely used in wells, tanks, and large containers for immersion purposes. They are designed for systems with high flow rates and are ideal for both domestic and light industrial applications. Submersible pumps are known for their quiet operation, low pressure spikes, and ease of installation.

Key Benefits:

Low maintenance requirements with long service life
Prevents and averts flooding damage
Powerful motor for enhanced performance
Long-term smooth and reliable operation
Multiple models for diverse applications

Borewell Pumps

Our Borewell Pumps are designed for deep well applications and are suitable for both residential and industrial use. These pumps are known for their efficiency in transferring water from deep wells to the surface, providing reliable performance even in challenging conditions.

Advantages:

Efficient handling of solids and liquids
Perfect for domestic and light industrial use
No priming required for operation
Quiet operation with stable pressure flow
Easy assembly with multiple configurations

Progressive Cavity Pumps

Progressive Cavity Pumps are ideal for handling highly viscous, abrasive, and shear-sensitive fluids. These pumps provide a smooth and pulsation-free flow and are excellent for applications requiring precise fluid movement.

Technical Features:

Wide Flow Range: 1 LPH to 1000 m³/hr
Self-Priming up to 9-meter water column
High Pressure: 1 bar to 240 bar
Viscosity: 1 cP to 3 million cP
Handles slurries with up to 70% solids
Suitable for complex fluid combinations

Key Applications:

Handles diverse fluid properties and combinations
Full-Service-In-Place (FSIP) design
High suction capability and reliability

Air Operated Diaphragm Pumps (AODD)

Our Air Operated Diaphragm Pumps (AODD) are designed for dosing and transferring thin to viscous fluids in a variety of industries, including general, chemical processing, food and beverage, and wastewater. These pumps offer low maintenance, energy efficiency, and ease of operation.

Performance Features:

Optimized mean time between repair (MTBR)
Simple and easy installation and use
Lube-free operation
Ideal for diverse industrial applications
Sanitary models for hygienic applications
Optimized energy efficiency

Swimming Pool Pumps

We offer a comprehensive range of swimming pool pumps for both residential and commercial pool applications. Our pumps are designed to ensure energy-efficient operation, optimized filtration quality, and low noise levels, ensuring crystal-clear water.

Key Applications:

Advanced pool filtration systems
Efficient water circulation
Energy-saving solutions
