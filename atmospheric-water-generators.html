Atmospheric Water Generators

Water from Air — Independent of Any Water Source

Atmospheric Water Generators (AWGs) use a refrigeration cycle to cool ambient air below its dew point, condensing moisture from the atmosphere into liquid water. The condensed water then passes through a multi-stage filtration and purification train — typically including sediment filtration, activated carbon, UV disinfection, and mineralisation — producing safe, great-tasting drinking water that meets WHO and IS 10500 standards.

Genoasis supplies AWG systems ranging from commercial units producing 100–500 litres per day for offices, schools, and resorts, through to large industrial and containerised systems generating 1,000 litres per day and above for remote communities, military bases, disaster relief operations, and arid-region infrastructure projects. Systems operate in relative humidity conditions as low as 30% RH.

Product Range

AWG System Types

Commercial AWG — 100–250 L/day

Freestanding commercial AWG units producing 100 to 250 litres of purified drinking water per day. Compact cabinet design suitable for offices, hotels, schools, and restaurants where mains water quality or supply is unreliable.

Commercial AWG — 500 L/day

Higher-output commercial units generating up to 500 litres per day for larger commercial premises, resorts, and community drinking water stations. Hot and cold dispensing options available with touchless dispensing for hygienic service.

Industrial AWG — 1,000 L/day

Industrial-scale AWG units producing 1,000 litres per day for remote sites, military and defence installations, island communities, and large hospitality or residential complexes without reliable water infrastructure.

Containerised AWG Systems

20-foot ISO container-mounted AWG systems for rapid deployment to disaster-affected areas, remote construction sites, and off-grid communities. Self-contained with solar or generator power options and internal water storage tanks.

Solar-Powered AWG Units

Off-grid AWG systems integrated with photovoltaic solar panels and battery storage for completely independent water production in areas without grid power. Ideal for arid rural villages and environmental monitoring stations.

AWG with Mineralisation & UV

Complete AWG systems with multi-stage post-treatment including re-mineralisation (calcium and magnesium), UV disinfection, and ozone preservation to produce balanced, healthy drinking water meeting all quality standards.

Why Choose Us

Key Features & Benefits

No Water Source Needed

AWGs produce water purely from atmospheric humidity, making them completely independent of wells, rivers, municipal supply, and water tankers — truly water security anywhere.

99.9% Pure Water Output

Multi-stage filtration, UV disinfection, and mineralisation produce drinking water that consistently meets WHO and IS 10500 quality standards with no chemicals added beyond mineralisation.

Solar Power Compatible

AWG units can be powered by solar PV systems with battery backup, enabling fully off-grid, sustainable water production in remote areas and communities underserved by infrastructure.

Rapid Deployment

Containerised and skid-mounted AWG systems are shipped ready to operate, requiring only power connection and commissioning — ideal for emergency response and temporary installations.
