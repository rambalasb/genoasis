Reverse Osmosis Membranes

Brackish Water, Seawater & Low-Energy RO Membrane Elements

Genoasis is a trusted supplier of spiral-wound reverse osmosis membrane elements for industrial, municipal and desalination water treatment. We stock 4-inch and 8-inch elements from leading international membrane manufacturers, covering brackish water, seawater (SWRO), low-energy, and fouling-resistant grades - so you get the right element for your feed water chemistry and target permeate quality.

Every membrane is matched to your application: send us your feed water analysis and required flow, and our engineers run a membrane projection to recommend the optimal element, array and operating pressure - and quote the matching FRP pressure vessels, antiscalants and cartridge filters in the same package.

RO membranes are supplied across India (Chennai, Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad), the Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait, Bahrain), and Africa (Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Ghana). We respond to every enquiry within 1 hour.

Product Range

RO Membrane Element Types

Brackish Water RO Membranes

High-rejection BWRO elements for borewell and surface water from 1,500-10,000 ppm TDS. Available in standard, low-energy and fouling-resistant grades. 99.5%+ stabilised salt rejection.

Seawater RO (SWRO) Membranes

High-pressure seawater elements with 99.7-99.8% salt rejection for single-pass desalination of feed up to 50,000 ppm TDS. Ideal for coastal and offshore desalination in the GCC and Africa.

Low-Energy RO Membranes

Energy-saving elements that deliver rated flux at reduced feed pressure, cutting high-pressure pump power consumption and operating cost on brackish water RO systems.

NF & UF Membranes

Nanofiltration elements for selective softening and organics/colour removal, and ultrafiltration modules for RO pre-treatment and clarification down to 0.02 microns.

FAQs

RO Membranes - Common Questions

Technical answers for RO plant engineers and buyers in India, the UAE and the GCC.

Which RO membrane brands and types does Genoasis supply?

We supply brackish water, seawater (SWRO), low-energy and fouling-resistant RO membranes, plus NF and UF membranes, from leading international manufacturers. Both 4-inch and 8-inch spiral-wound elements are stocked, along with 2.5-inch elements for small systems.

What salt rejection do your seawater RO membranes achieve?

Our seawater RO (SWRO) membranes achieve stabilised salt rejection of 99.7% to 99.8%, suitable for single-pass desalination of feed water up to 45,000-50,000 ppm TDS. Brackish water RO membranes achieve 99.5%+ rejection at lower operating pressures.

Can you help select the right RO membrane for my feed water?

Yes. Send us your feed water analysis (TDS, ions, temperature, SDI) and required permeate flow. Our engineers run a membrane projection to recommend the optimal element type, array configuration and operating pressure, and quote the membranes and matching pressure vessels together.

Do you supply RO membranes to the UAE, Saudi Arabia and Africa?

Yes. We supply RO membranes across the Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait, Bahrain), India, and Africa (Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Ghana, Tanzania, Ethiopia). We handle export documentation and shipping, and respond to enquiries within 1 hour.
