Dunnage Air Bags

Genoasis offers high-quality, cost-effective dunnage air bags designed for safe and secure transportation of goods. Our professional staff ensures that your materials are safely transported and properly installed at your facility.

Dunnage air bags play a crucial role in securing and stabilizing cargo within containers, closed rail cars, trucks, and ships. Unlike traditional wood blocking and bracing, they effectively prevent both lateral and longitudinal movements during transit. Choosing the right dunnage bag depends on several factors including void size, cargo height, and specific shipping needs.

At Genoasis, we offer a diverse range of sizes to cater to various requirements.

Features of Container Dunnage Air Bags

Material: Made from durable materials such as polyethylene (PE) or woven polypropylene (PP), providing strength and resistance to punctures and tears
Inflation: Inflated using compressed air through valves or ports. Can be inflated quickly and easily with manual or pneumatic pumps
Size Variety: Available in various sizes and shapes to fit different void spaces within shipping containers, tailored to specific cargo requirements
Strength and Load Support: Designed to withstand substantial pressure and forces from cargo shifting during transport, providing effective load securing
Reusable: PP bags are reusable, allowing for multiple shipments before replacement. Can be deflated, compacted, and stored for future use
Compatibility: Compatible with various types of cargo, including fragile or irregularly shaped items, providing versatile protection
Environmental Impact: Generally more environmentally friendly compared to alternatives like wood, as they are recyclable and reduce deforestation
Quick Installation: Easy and fast installation process that reduces loading time and improves operational efficiency compared to traditional blocking methods
