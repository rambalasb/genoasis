Membrane Products

Membrane Products

Precision membranes for every water quality challenge.

Genoasis supplies a complete portfolio of spiral-wound and hollow-fibre membrane elements from leading global manufacturers. Our RO membrane elements achieve salt rejection rates above 99.7% for seawater applications and above 99.5% for brackish water, delivering consistently high-purity permeate across varying feed water conditions.

From nanofiltration for hardness removal to ultrafiltration for turbidity and pathogen reduction, and MBR membranes for advanced biological wastewater treatment, our range covers standard 2.5", 4", and 8" diameters. We carry both replacement elements and full membrane system designs tailored to your flow and quality targets.

Beyond the GCC and India, Genoasis supplies membrane elements across Africa - including Nigeria (Lagos, Abuja), Kenya (Nairobi), South Africa (Johannesburg, Cape Town), Ghana (Accra), Tanzania (Dar es Salaam), and Ethiopia (Addis Ababa). We support water treatment projects at every scale, from small community RO systems to large industrial desalination and process water plants.

Membrane Element Types

Brackish Water RO Membranes

Polyamide thin-film composite elements rated for feed TDS up to 10,000 ppm. Available in 4" × 40" and 8" × 40" configurations with stabilised salt rejection exceeding 99.5% and low operating pressure for energy savings.

Seawater RO Membranes

High-pressure elements designed for seawater feed with TDS up to 45,000 ppm, achieving salt rejection above 99.7%. Robust construction resists biofouling and oxidative damage in harsh marine environments.

Nanofiltration Membranes

NF membranes for selective removal of divalent ions, hardness, colour, and organic micropollutants. Operate at lower pressures than RO, reducing energy consumption while maintaining high rejection of target contaminants.

Ultrafiltration Membranes

Hollow-fibre UF modules with MWCO from 10,000 to 150,000 Daltons for turbidity, suspended solids, bacteria, and virus removal. Ideal as RO pre-treatment or standalone tertiary polishing stages.

MBR Membranes

Submerged hollow-fibre MBR membrane modules for membrane bioreactor wastewater treatment plants. Deliver effluent quality suitable for direct discharge or reclamation, with high mixed liquor tolerance and easy in-situ cleaning.

Low Energy RO Membranes

Next-generation low-energy elements optimised for tap and well water applications. Deliver full salt rejection at significantly reduced operating pressures, cutting energy consumption and running costs in municipal and commercial systems.

Pressure Vessels & FRP Filters

RO Pressure Vessels & FRP Filters

Our fibre-reinforced polymer (FRP) pressure vessels are designed to house standard 4", 8", and 16" diameter RO membrane elements, delivering outstanding structural integrity at operating pressures up to 600 psi. The smooth internal liner prevents biofilm accumulation and provides years of trouble-free performance in seawater, brackish water, and high-TDS applications.

In addition to membrane housings, we supply FRP multi-media and sand filter vessels for pre-treatment stages. Available in a wide range of diameters and with top or side-mount port configurations, these vessels are the backbone of reliable RO and filtration systems worldwide.

FRP pressure vessels and filter tanks are supplied to water treatment projects across Africa, including Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Ghana, and Tanzania. FRP's inherent corrosion resistance makes it the material of choice in humid tropical climates common across Sub-Saharan Africa, where steel vessels corrode rapidly.

String Wound Polypropylene Cartridges

Depth-filtration cartridges with graded density wound construction for high dirt-holding capacity. Available in 1–100 micron ratings; economical and widely used in RO pre-treatment and general industrial filtration.

Melt Blown Polypropylene Cartridges

Carbon Block Cartridges

Polypropylene Filter Housings

Stainless Steel Multi-Cartridge Housings

Media & Resin Types

Silica Sand

High-purity quartz silica sand with SiO₂ content above 95%, available in grades from 0.3–0.6 mm up to 1.0–2.0 mm. Washed, dried, and screened to tight tolerances for consistent filtration bed performance.

Anthracite Coal Media

Crushed and graded anthracite coal (0.8–2.0 mm) for use as the top layer in dual-media and multi-media filter beds. Lower density than sand allows deeper particle penetration and longer filtration runs before backwash.

AFM Activated Filter Media

Activated Filter Media (AFM) manufactured from recycled glass. Biologically resistant surface prevents biofilm formation, offering superior filtration to sand at equivalent bed depths with up to 30% energy savings.

Activated Carbon (GAC & PAC)

Granular activated carbon (GAC) and powdered activated carbon (PAC) for chlorine, organics, taste, odour, and trace contaminant removal. Coal-based, coconut shell, and wood-based grades available for specific applications.

SAFF & MBBR Biofilm Carriers

Plastic polyethylene biofilm carrier media for submerged aerated fixed film (SAFF) and moving bed biofilm reactor (MBBR) biological wastewater treatment systems. High surface area, buoyant in aerated tanks.

Ion Exchange Resins (SAC, SBA, Mixed Bed)

Food-grade and industrial-grade strongly acidic cation, strongly basic anion, and mixed bed resins for water softening, dealkalisation, deionisation, and ultra-pure water production. NSF certified grades available.

Common Questions

RO Membrane FAQs

What types of RO membranes does Genoasis supply?

We supply brackish water RO (BWRO) membranes for feed TDS up to 10,000 ppm, seawater RO (SWRO) membranes for TDS up to 45,000 ppm, nanofiltration (NF) for hardness and colour removal, ultrafiltration (UF) hollow-fibre modules, MBR membranes for wastewater treatment, and low-energy RO elements. Available in 2.5", 4", and 8" diameters.

Do you supply SWRO membranes for desalination plants in UAE and Saudi Arabia?

Yes. We supply high-pressure seawater RO membrane elements with 99.7%+ salt rejection specifically engineered for SWRO desalination plants across UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, and Oman. These membranes are built to withstand the high chloride and temperature conditions of Gulf seawater.

Can you supply replacement membrane elements for existing RO systems in India?

Yes. Genoasis specialises in replacement RO membrane elements for existing systems across India - Chennai, Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, and beyond. Share your current membrane specs (brand, model, 4" or 8") and we'll recommend compatible replacement elements with competitive pricing and fast delivery.

How long do RO membranes last and when should they be replaced?

With correct antiscalant dosing and regular CIP cleaning, RO membranes last 3–5 years. Replace when normalised permeate flow drops more than 15%, salt rejection falls below 98% (BWRO) or 99.5% (SWRO), or pressure differential rises significantly. We also supply membrane cleaners and antiscalants to maximise membrane service life.

What is the minimum order quantity for membrane elements?

There is no minimum order for stocked elements - we supply single elements through to full container quantities. For large-scale projects, we offer volume pricing and technical consultation for complete membrane system design. Contact us to discuss your project volume and timeline.

Do you supply RO membranes to African countries?

Yes. Genoasis supplies RO, UF and MBR membrane elements across Africa, including Nigeria (Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt), Kenya (Nairobi, Mombasa), South Africa (Johannesburg, Cape Town, Durban), Ghana (Accra), Tanzania (Dar es Salaam), and Ethiopia (Addis Ababa). We ship via established freight routes and can consolidate membrane orders with other water treatment components to reduce shipping costs. Lead times and shipping options vary by destination - contact us for a quotation for your specific country and project.
